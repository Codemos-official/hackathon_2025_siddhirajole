About Export Launchpad

Export Launchpad is a web application designed to empower small Indian exporters to confidently enter international markets. It addresses the challenges faced by beginners, micro-exporters, and small businesses who often struggle with product selection, profit calculation, risk assessment, and understanding export procedures.

The platform combines four key modules:

  • Niche Product Finder: Identify high-demand, export-ready products.
  • Profit Snapshot: Estimate potential profits and break-even points.
  • Risk Radar: Assess payment, regulatory, and logistics risks.
  • Smart Docs: Step-by-step document checklist for exporting.

With an intuitive interface and actionable insights, Export Launchpad makes global trade accessible and understandable, helping new exporters make data-driven decisions, minimize risks, and grow their business successfully.
